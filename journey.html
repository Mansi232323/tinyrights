Are you curious about what rights mean and how you deserve to be treated well, safe, and have your needs met? Join us at this level to learn the basic concepts of rights and discover the amazing world of understanding and respect.

14 minutes

As you progress, you'll recognize specific rights like education, play, and protection. Learn about who protects these rights and how you can seek help when needed. It's like being an apprentice in the world of rights!

12 minutes

Ready to take your knowledge to the next level? Here, you'll understand broader rights like freedom of expression, equality, and participating in decisions that affect you. It's all about respecting others' rights too!

15 minutes

Now, let's dive deep into the intricate details of rights, exploring concepts like privacy, non-discrimination, and the right to a healthy environment. You'll even understand how laws and policies support these rights.

8 minutes
